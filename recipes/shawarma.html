Chicken Shawarma Rice

The most delicious shawarma chicken you'll ever eat! Serve with rice, tomato and cucumber slices.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 cup of cooked rice

Steps

  1. Cook chicken.
  2. Dice tomatoes and cucumber while waiting occassionally checking the chicken.
  3. Prepare the rice on the plate with the earlier vegetables
  4. Add chicken and enjoy
